10 EARLSHALL

The next morning,John took Ginger and I to Earshall Park.John and Mr York,our new groom,took us to a large stable.We were happy to be next to each other.
John said to Mr York,These horses like a long rein.
But Mr York replied,"Here we use a short rein.My lady wants herhorses to hold their heads high."
John came to pat and speak to us for the last time.His voice was very sad.
I put my face up to him to say goodbye,and then he left.
At three o'clock in the afternoon Mr York took us round to the front of the house.It was very grand.Soon the lady of the house came and looked at us.She said nothing,and got into the carriage.This was the first time I wore a short rein.And it was very uncomfortable.
The next day the lady said,"York,you must put those horses'heads higher."
That day we had to go up a huge hill.I tried to pull the carriage along,but I had to pull with my head up.It was hard and it hurt my back and legs.
After that our reins got shorter and shorter every day.I hated it.And things got worse after that.
One day Mr York fixed the rein very tight.It was terrible.Ginger didn't like it at all.She reared up.York hit his nose and his hat fell off.She reared again,and kicked.Then she fell down,kicking me too.I stood,angry,with a sore leg,my head still high.I was very miserable.
They didn't put Ginger into the carriage again.But one of the Lord's sons wanted to use her for hunting.As for me,I had a new partner called Max.But I had to wear that short rein for four long months.When I finished work my neck and chest hurt,my mouth and tongue were sore,and I felt tired and sad.
In my old home Birtwick Park I always felt that John and Squire Gordon were my friends,but here,I had no friends at all.

中文：

10 厄尔肖庄园

第二天早上，约翰带着我和金格去厄尔肖庄园。约翰和约克先生，我们的新马夫，把我们带到一个大马厩。我们很高兴能在一起。
约翰对约克先生说，这些马喜欢长缰绳。
但约克先生回答说:“在这里我们使用短缰绳。爵士夫人希望她的马能昂首挺胸。”
约翰轻拍我们，最后一次对我们讲话。他的声音很悲伤。
我把脸凑到他面前说再见，然后他就走了。
下午三点钟，约克先生带我们绕到房子前面。它非常宏伟。不久，女主人走过来看着我们。她什么也没说，上了马车。这是我第一次穿短缰绳。非常不舒服。
第二天，那位女士说:“约克，你必须把马的头抬得高一点。”
那天我们必须爬一座大山。我想拉着马车走，但不得不抬起头来拉。它是困难的，它伤害了我的背部和腿。
从那以后，我们的缰绳一天比一天短。我讨厌它。在那之后，情况变得更糟。
一天，约克先生把缰绳绑得很紧。这是可怕的。金格一点也不喜欢。她用后腿直立起来。约克撞到了鼻子，帽子掉了下来。她又直立起来，踢了起来。然后她摔倒了，也踢我。我愤怒地站着，腿酸痛，头仍然高着。我非常痛苦。
他们没有再把金格塞进马车。但领主的一个儿子想用她打猎。至于我，我有个新搭档叫麦克斯。但我得戴着缰绳整整四个月。当我完成工作时，我的脖子和胸部疼，我的嘴和舌头疼，我感到疲惫和悲伤。
在我的老家伯特威克公园，我总觉得约翰和乡绅戈登是我的朋友，但在这里，我一个朋友也没有。
